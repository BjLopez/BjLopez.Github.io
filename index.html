About Me

I am a graphic designer based in Massachusetts. I started off as a traditional artist using pen, pencil, paint and ink on paper. I started graphic design and web development in 2021-2022 to try out something new and expand on my work. My favorite programs to work with is Photoshop and Illustrator as I couldn't stop myself wanting to work on these programs to work on digital illustrations and collages. I also use Procreate to make digital artwork with some translated through handdrawn sketches.
