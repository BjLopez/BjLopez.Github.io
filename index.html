About Me

I am a graphic designer based in Massachusetts. I started off as a traditional artist using pen, pencil, paint and ink on paper. I started graphic design and web development in 2021-2022 to try out something new and expand on my work. My some of my favorite programs to work with is Photoshop and Illustrator as I couldn't stop myself wanting to work on these programs to work on digital illustrations and collages. I also use Procreate to make digital artwork with some translated through handdrawn sketches to make character designs and concepts.


Origin

My inspration draws from the expirences I've had, the forms of media I enjoyed, the principles and morals. I believe as well the events I've read or witnessed. What furthe inspired me is the most is Japanese media such as anime and manga through the thematic storytelling, expressions, and scenes that I want to capture onto a platform to implement on my own.
