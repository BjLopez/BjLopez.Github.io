About Me

I am a graphic designer based in Massachusetts. I started off as a traditional artist using pen, pencil, paint and ink on paper. I started graphic design and web development in 2021-2022 to try out something new and expand on my work. My some of my favorite programs to work with is Photoshop and Illustrator as I couldn't stop myself wanting to work on these programs to work on digital illustrations and collages. I also use Procreate to make digital artwork with some translated through handdrawn sketches to make character designs and concepts.


Origin

My inspration draws from the expirences I've had, the forms of media I enjoyed, the principles and moral systems I believe as well the events I've read or witnessed. What furthe inspired me is the most is Japanese media such as anime and manga through the thematic storytelling, expressions, and scenes that I want to capture onto a platform to implement on my own which is prevelent in my artwork.

Jojo Rabbit, directed by Taika Waititi Jojo Rabbit is an anti-hate satire that takes place in World War II-era Germany. The story is about a boy named Jojo whose imaginary friend is Adolf Hitler and is a devout follower of him and even a member of the Hitler’s Youth. He comes home to discover a Jewish girl named Elsa whom his mother is hiding within the wall. Jojo’s mother wants him to enjoy his childhood than being obsessed with war. He familiarizes with Elsa and befriends her and but Hitler attempts to push him away. The subject of the piece is Jojo breaking through a swastika and shattering it which symbolizes his development as a character. The palette of black, white, and red is taken from nazi iconography and it is satirical in nature but tells an anti-hate narrative so I decided to parody such a hateful symbolism.
